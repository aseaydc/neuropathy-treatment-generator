‚úã Digit Span Test (Eyes Closed)

Important: All digit span tests must be performed with patient's eyes closed to eliminate visual compensation. Test light touch discrimination, sustained hold awareness, and digit identification.
Severity Assessment: If a limb fails ALL THREE tests (incorrect/unable on span & hold, plus 0-2/5 on digit ID), this indicates SEVERE sensory deficit.

Hands Testing

Light Touch - Span Identification

Touch two fingers. Patient reports how many fingers between the touches.

Sustained Hold Test

Hold finger without lifting. Patient maintains awareness of contact.

Digit Numbering Test

Hold specific digit. Patient identifies which number (1-5).

Feet Testing

Light Touch - Span Identification

Touch two toes. Patient reports how many toes between the touches.

Sustained Hold Test

Hold toe without lifting. Patient maintains awareness of contact.

Toe Numbering Test

Hold specific toe. Patient identifies which number (1-5).
